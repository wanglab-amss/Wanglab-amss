POSITIONS OPENINGS

We are looking for enthusiastic undergraduate students, graduate students, and post-doctoral fellows to join our optimization & computational systems biology & bioinformatics research group in the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Sciences at Chinese Academy of Mathematics.

Graduate students

For undergraduate and graduate students, please send a resume to ywang@amss.ac.cn.

Motivated students with strong mathematics, statistics and computer science interests are welcome to join us. Details can be found in the Education Department of the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, CAS. For Phd candidate, you may find useful resource for the entrance exams.

Postdoc

To apply for post-doctoral positions, please send a CV to ywang@amss.ac.cn that includes a list of publications and details of 2-3 references.

A postdoc candidate should have strong interestes in Operations Research/Complex Networks/Bioinformatics/Systems Biology. You can have a PhD degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or Biology. It will be great if your dissertation is related to Computational Biology and Bioinformatics or other Data Science fields. Strong programming skills and good oral and written communication skills in English are necessary.
